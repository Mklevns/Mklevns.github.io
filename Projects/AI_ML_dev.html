Different quantitative metrics exist to define "fairness," but they are often mutually exclusive, making the choice of metric an ethical and contextual decision, not just a technical one.

Post-Deployment Monitoring

Once in production, a model's performance can degrade. This phenomenon, known as **model drift**, requires continuous monitoring.

Data Drift

The statistical properties of the input data change. For example, a loan prediction model's inputs (like average income) drift as the economy changes.

Concept Drift

The relationship between inputs and the output changes. For example, fraudsters develop new techniques, making old patterns obsolete.
